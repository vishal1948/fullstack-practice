Shivaji Tarun Mandal

Shivaji Tarun Mandal is one of the most renowned football teams in Kolhapur. Known for its disciplined training and strong performances in local tournaments, the team has a rich history and a strong fan base.

The team has participated in numerous regional and national-level competitions, showcasing their talent and sportsmanship. Their commitment to excellence has made them a symbol of pride for Kolhapur.
