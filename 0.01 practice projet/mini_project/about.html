To create an online presence, showcase the history, philosophy's cultural significance of Kolhapur talims. concept of "Talim's" came from the wrestling culture of Kolhapur. Shree Shahu Maharaj of Kolhapur has a significant role in this culture. They used to appropriate and give reward to the well established and renowed Wrestler. As per the time this talim's and mandals changed to the regional and subreginal groups and Friends circle. And they adopted the culture of Ganesh Utsar Festival as prime. The main objective of this change is to get togather all around the people and form a good socity. As per the time this talims can mandale adopted the used to arrange, big sport culture and Tournaments in which all around Sport team in Kolhapur used to Participate and Compete with each other.
